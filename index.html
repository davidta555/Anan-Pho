    Are there any commitments?

    Nope. You can skip your weekly delivery or cancel at any time.

    Just be sure to do so by your cutoff: 11:59 pm est 5 days before your delivery

    What if I'm not home?

    If you’re not at home, don’t worry!

    Delivery times are from 8 am to 8 pm on the day you choose. You can always add special delivery instructions to your account. You can also change your delivery day online if you need to.

    How will my food stay cool?

    We carefully handpack all ingredients with special ice packs and insulation

    So your food keeps cool until you get home.

    What if I have food allergies or intolerances?

    We state our ingredients on the menu section.

    If you have a serious food intolerance then we recomend not ordering from us.
